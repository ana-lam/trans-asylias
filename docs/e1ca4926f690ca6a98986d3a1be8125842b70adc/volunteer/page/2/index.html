Volunteer



As we are building the TRANS ASYLIAS foundation and family around the world we are calling for volunteers to join us.


Learn more about volunteering opportunities on this page and sign up using the link below!



  • Volunteer Registration

Whether you live in New York and can meet and work closely with us at Trans Asylias, or anywhere else in the world just a virtual meeting away, you can be of tremendous help.

TRANS ASYLIAS urgently needs:

Case Management Volunteers for:

  • Protégés Intake
    Review requests for help, schedule calls to discuss their situation, assess if their case is relevant to our work, and if not direct them to other resources.
    Languages needed: English, Arabic, Dari Persian, Spanish.
  • Relationship Management
    Each case we take becomes our Protégé and is allocated a case manager who can find the time to regularly speak to the Protégé. Progress on the case is reported to and discussed with directors.
    Priority given to volunteers with a background in trauma-informed care, crisis counseling and skills in navigating sensitive conversations.
  • Mental Health Care

    • – Trauma-Informed Care: Volunteers who have training or experience in providing care for individuals in crisis.

    – Counselors / Therapists: Pro-bono 30-60 mns sessions for our Protégés.
    Languages: Arabic, English.

  • Social Media

    • – Account Manager: Volunteer who can manage the socials for TRANS ASYLIAS (Instagram, Twitter, Facebook).

    – Content Creator: Volunteer with Photoshop / Canva experience.

  • Press / Communications
    Volunteer, ideally with background in PR, to develop relationships with the press and all necessary media tools.
  • Research

    • – Detail-oriented volunteers who love to spend time on the internet and assist us with:

    ▪ Knowledge of laws regarding LGBTQI+ discriminations and protections in relevant countries.

    ▪ Building directory and network of Trans, LGBTQI+, Asylum Seekers organizations worldwide.

    ▪ Researching small grants and fundraising opportunities.

  • Grant Writers
  • Legal Assistance
    Pro-Bono Lawyers, Legal Professionals, Law Students for

    • – Reporting about immigrations laws

    – Working with refugees and asylum seekers.

    – Securing legal protections for TRANS ASYLIAS, its directors, employees and volunteers.
